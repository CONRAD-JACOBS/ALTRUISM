Instructions

Along with other robots in the lab, Zeek is participating in an informal competition to become the lead lab robot.

Each robot has a score that represents the number of visual search tasks humans have successfully completed on its behalf.

The robot with the highest score at the end of the semester will win.

You now have an opportunity to contribute to Zeek's score by completing more visual search tasks.

Contributing to Zeek's score is entirely optional. You may press ESC to quit at any time without compromising your data.

As soon as you are finished, please let the experimenter know.
